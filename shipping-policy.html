Shipping Policy

Last Updated: 4-10-2024

Thank you for visiting and shopping at dotcombubble. The following terms and conditions constitute our Shipping Policy.

1. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 4-5 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or public holidays.

If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. Please allow additional days in transit for delivery. If there will be a significant delay in the shipment of your order, we will contact you via Instagram at @dtcmbbl_.

2. Shipping Charges and Delivery Estimates

Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout. Estimated delivery times are as follows:

  • Standard Shipping: 2-3 business days after processing

Delivery times may vary depending on external factors beyond our control, such as shipping delays or public holidays.

3. Shipping Locations

We currently ship to most locations within India. At this time, we do not offer international shipping or ship to P.O. boxes, APO, or FPO addresses.

4. Shipment Confirmation & Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a shipment confirmation message via Instagram or email, along with your tracking number(s). The tracking number will be active within 24 hours.

5. Customs, Duties, and Taxes

dotcombubble is not responsible for any customs or taxes applied to your order. All fees imposed during or after shipping are the responsibility of the customer (tariffs, taxes, etc.).

6. Damages

dotcombubble is not liable for any products damaged or lost during shipping. If you receive your order damaged, please contact the shipment carrier to file a claim. Please save all packaging materials and damaged goods before filing a claim.

7. Return Policy

Our Return Policy can be found on our Terms and Conditions page. Please refer to it for details on how to return or exchange products.
